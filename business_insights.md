# Business Insights

## Top Product Category

Beauty & Health generated the highest revenue among all product categories, indicating strong customer demand in this segment.

## Best Sales Month

November 2017 recorded the highest sales revenue, suggesting seasonal shopping trends and promotional activity contributed to increased purchases.

## Customer Satisfaction

The majority of customers gave 5-star review scores, reflecting a high level of customer satisfaction and positive shopping experiences.

## Regional Performance

São Paulo (SP) contributed the highest revenue among all states, making it the most important market for the business.

## Recommendations

* Increase inventory for top-performing product categories.
* Focus marketing campaigns during peak sales months.
* Strengthen customer retention programs to maintain high satisfaction levels.
* Expand targeted promotions in high-performing states such as São Paulo.
